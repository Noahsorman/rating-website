Finch

A.K.A - Hidous Harald, 4 Wheeler


Is one of Skrallgaming's founders and an old-timer in the smash community, even playing before the SS3B times during the Melee days with Mikka, LTR and Slak1. However, he somewhat faded out during the SS3B times and never fully learned the pace of the new games. His lack of experience in the new games luckily got carried by his experience in the old one, placing him astonishing 2nd! A dream-run with his partner Chagzter at SSE-LI 2017. But once others caught up (Izizaki and Barre) he got stabilized at rank #5.
Who carried who? Between Finch and Chagzter?
