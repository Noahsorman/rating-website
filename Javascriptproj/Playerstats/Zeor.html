Zeor

A.K.A - Babehunter


Joined SG in late SS3B times, when ECDM opened. Zeor picked up Toon Link at SSE-MI 2016 where he played 1-2 against LTR, won against Miley who'd won over Izizaki and finished the tournament 4th. At SSE-LI 2017 he teamed up with Jäger and defeated Anttown but later finished 5th. Later the same year at SSE-II he had an even worse run and finished last, though with a very unlucky bracket. Zeor, much like Barre is very good individually, but seem to lack firm team play.
Will Zeor climb or fall from his median rating?
