MIKKA

A.K.A - Mikka Tronkinen, Steeve The Speed


Is one of Skrallgaming's founders and a veteran in the smash community since the SS3B times. He's currently ranked #1 and is one of two players above 1600 rating and may because of that be considered one of two gods. Mikka is a fan of lightweighted characters such as Mewtwo, Jigglypuff and Ness, which fits his plastyle of wanting to fight off-stage. Although, in a 2v2-format with no 'team damage' he's said that he prefers heavier characters which is why he played Ike through most of 2017.
